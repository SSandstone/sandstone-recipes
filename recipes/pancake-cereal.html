Pancake Cereal

Description

What's better than pancakes? Nothing, you say? How about tiny little mini-pancakes in a bowl like cereal, topped with maple syrup, cinnamon, and butter? Does that pique your interest?

As a kid, I grew up with Cookie Krisps, Reese's Peanut Butter Cups, and Oreo-whatever cereal. It would've been heaven... if my parents ever let me have any. In come pancakes. A special treat with just as much sugar as that cereal but for some reason much more socially respected as a breakfast food. Go figure. It wasn't until much later that I started making pancake style cereal, but it brings back all the glory of a sweet breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup buckwheat fl
  • 2 cups milk
  • 4 eggs
  • bit o salt
  • oil
  • 2 pears
  • 1 cup water
  • (Optional) 1 tbsp chopped ginger
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp honey

Steps

  1. peel and chop pears
  2. add pears, water, ginger, cinnam, honey to sauce pan and simmer 10 mins
  3. mash pear pieces
  4. mix eggs, milk, flour and salt
  5. maka da pancakes, cover whole pan thin w batter
